Annual Thanksgiving Celebration at Mercy Once Again Ministry

Date: August 31, 2024

Location: Church Hall

Description: Each year, Mercy Once Again Ministry comes together to celebrate the Annual Thanksgiving—a time of gratitude, fellowship, and spiritual reflection. This cherished event is not just a tradition but a significant gathering where the church community reflects on the blessings of the past year and expresses heartfelt thanks to God. The Annual Thanksgiving celebration is marked by uplifting worship services, powerful sermons, and vibrant music, all designed to inspire and uplift the congregation. Members and guests alike are invited to partake in this joyous occasion, where testimonies of God’s goodness are shared, and the spirit of gratitude is palpable. The day is filled with various activities, including a special offering of thanks, communal meals, and opportunities to serve the less fortunate, embodying the spirit of giving that defines this event. It is a time for families to come together, for friends to reconnect, and for the entire church to unite in praise and thanksgiving. Join us for this year’s Annual Thanksgiving at Mercy Once Again Ministry—a day dedicated to giving thanks, fostering community, and celebrating the abundant blessings in our lives.
