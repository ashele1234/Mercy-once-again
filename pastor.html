Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Olarinde Olufemi Emmanuel.

Senior Pastor, Endless Mercy Once Again

Background:

Pastor Olarinde Olufemi Emmanuel has been a dedicated servant of God for over 20 years, bringing a wealth of experience, wisdom, and compassion to Endless Mercy Once Again Church. With a deep calling to ministry that began early in life, Pastor Olarinde pursued theological education at Life Theological Seminary, where they earned [degree or certification], equipping them to lead and shepherd a growing congregation.

Ministry Vision:

Pastor Olarinde is driven by a passion for discipleship, outreach, and spiritual growth. Their vision for Endless Mercy Once Again Church is to create a community where every member experiences the transformative power of God's mercy. Pastor Olarinde believes in preaching the unfiltered Word of God, fostering deep, meaningful relationships within the church, and extending the church’s reach into the wider community through acts of service and love.

Achievements and Impact:

Under Pastor Olarinde's leadership, Endless Mercy Once Again Church has seen significant growth, not only in numbers but in spiritual depth.Key achievements include: - Expanding Outreach Programs: Pastor Olarinde spearheaded several community initiatives, including envavelism, crusades, which have touched the lives of many and demonstrated the church’s commitment to service.
-Enhanced Worship Experience:
Through innovative worship formats and a focus on dynamic, Spirit-led services, Pastor Olarinde has enriched the church’s worship experience, making it more accessible and impactful for all attendees.
- Discipleship and Mentorship:
Pastor Olarinde has mentored numerous young leaders within the church, guiding them to discover and develop their own spiritual gifts and leadership abilities.

Personal Life:

Outside the pulpit, Pastor Olarinde Olufemi Emmanuel is a devoted man, married to Pastor (Mrs) Olarinde Mary oluwafunmi, with whom they have 3 children. The family is actively involved in the life of the church, sharing their love for ministry and service. In their free time, Pastor Olarinde enjoys Reading and traveling, which provides a balance to their busy pastoral duties.

A Message from Pastor Olarinde:

My greatest joy is witnessing the power of God’s mercy in the lives of our congregation. At Endless Mercy Once Again Church, we are a family rooted in love, guided by faith, and committed to serving our community. My prayer for each of you is that you experience the fullness of God’s mercy and grace in your life, and that together, we will continue to build a church that reflects Christ’s love to the world. Pastor Olarinde Olufemi Emmanuel.
