About Endless Mercy Once Again

Endless Mercy Once Again Ministry is a place where God’s boundless love and grace are at the heart of everything we do. Our church is a welcoming community of believers committed to living out the teachings of Christ through worship, fellowship, and service.

Our mission

Our mission is to extend God’s endless mercy to all who seek it. We strive to be a beacon of hope, sharing the love of Christ with our community and beyond. Through our worship services, outreach programs, and spiritual guidance, we aim to lead people into a deeper relationship with God and a life filled with purpose and meaning.

Worship With Us

At Endless Mercy Once Again Church, we gather regularly to worship, pray, and seek God’s presence. Our services are filled with uplifting music, inspiring messages, and heartfelt prayer. We believe in the power of collective worship and invite everyone to join us as we experience the transformative power of God’s mercy.

Our community

We are more than just a church; we are a family. Our community is made up of people from all walks of life who come together to support one another in faith and love. Whether you’re new to the area or looking for a spiritual home, you’ll find a warm welcome at Endless Mercy Once Again Church.

Endless Mercy Programs

One of our key programs is the Endless Mercy Once Again prayer sessions, held every month from the 5th to the 12th, between 11:00 PM and 1:00 AM. These powerful nights of prayer are dedicated to seeking God’s mercy, healing, and breakthroughs. We gather as a community to lift our voices in prayer, trusting that God’s mercy is new every morning.
